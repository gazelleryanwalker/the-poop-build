# Personal Injury Law

At The Costello Law Group, we understand that suffering a personal injury can be one of the most challenging experiences of your life. Our dedicated team of personal injury attorneys is here to fight for your rights and ensure you receive the compensation you deserve.

## Our Personal Injury Services

We handle a wide range of personal injury cases, including:

### Auto Accidents
Car accidents, truck accidents, motorcycle accidents, and pedestrian accidents. We work with accident reconstruction experts to build strong cases.

### Slip and Fall
Premises liability cases involving dangerous conditions on someone else's property. Property owners have a duty to maintain safe conditions.

### Medical Malpractice
When healthcare providers fail to meet the standard of care, resulting in injury or death. We work with medical experts to prove negligence.

### Product Liability
Injuries caused by defective or dangerous products. Manufacturers must ensure their products are safe for consumers.

### Wrongful Death
When negligence results in the loss of a loved one. We help families seek justice and compensation for their devastating loss.

## What to Do After an Injury

1. **Seek Medical Attention** - Your health is the priority
2. **Document Everything** - Photos, witnesses, medical records
3. **Don't Talk to Insurance Companies** - Let us handle all communication
4. **Contact Us Immediately** - The sooner we start, the better

## Compensation You May Be Entitled To

- Medical expenses (past and future)
- Lost wages and loss of earning capacity
- Pain and suffering
- Emotional distress
- Property damage
- Punitive damages (in cases of gross negligence)
