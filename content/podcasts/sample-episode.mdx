## Welcome to Our Podcast

This is a sample episode to help you understand the podcast structure. You can edit or delete this through the TinaCMS admin interface at `/admin`.

### What You'll Learn

In future episodes, we'll discuss:

- Personal injury case insights
- Legal tips for accident victims
- Insurance claim strategies
- Client success stories

### Getting Started

To add your own podcasts:

1. Go to `/admin` in your browser
2. Click on "Podcasts" in the sidebar
3. Create a new episode or edit this one
4. Add your audio file URL
5. Write your show notes
6. Uncheck "Draft" when ready to publish

### Important Notes

- The `draft` field keeps episodes hidden from the public site
- Add your audio file URL in the `audioUrl` field
- Episode numbers help organize your content
- Tags make episodes easier to find

Happy podcasting!
