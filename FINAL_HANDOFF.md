## How to Use TinaCMS

### Accessing the CMS

1.  Go to [https://thecostellolawgroup.com/admin](https://thecostellolawgroup.com/admin)
2.  Click **Log in**
3.  Authenticate with your GitHub account

### Visual Editing

Once you are logged in, you will be in visual editing mode. You can navigate to any page on your website and edit the content directly on the page. Simply click on the content you want to edit, make your changes in the sidebar, and click **Save**. Your changes will be automatically committed to your GitHub repository and deployed to your live website within minutes.
